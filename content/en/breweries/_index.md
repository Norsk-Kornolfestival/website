## Breweries 2026

### Klostergarden Bryggeri

**Tautra island, near Trondheim** · [klostergardentautra.no](https://klostergardentautra.no)

Norwegian farmhouse ale using Stjørdal-style malts. Pub, restaurant, brewery, and accommodation.

### Bygland Bryggeri

**Setesdal** · [byglandbryggeri.no](https://byglandbryggeri.no)

Kveik fermentation with local ingredients; experimental beers and sake.

### Lom Bryggeri

**Grjotheim farm, Lom** · [lomb.no](https://lomb.no)

Founded 2016. Self-picked berries/herbs, ancient hop strain, local barley malting.

### Brulandselva

**Hornindal** · [lokalmat.no](https://lokalmat.no)

Collaboration with Terje Raftevold; fermented with Terje's kveik; brewed at De Proef Brouweri.
