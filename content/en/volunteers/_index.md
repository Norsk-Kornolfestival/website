## Become a volunteer

The Norwegian Kornøl Festival is a non-profit event run entirely by volunteers. We always need help — from setup and serving to cleanup after the festival.

As a volunteer you get:

- Free festival access
- Food and drinks during your shifts
- A unique behind-the-scenes experience

### Sign up

Contact us at [post@norskkornolfestival.no](mailto:post@norskkornolfestival.no) or follow us on [Facebook](https://www.facebook.com/kornfestival) for updates on volunteer registration.
