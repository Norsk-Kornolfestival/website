We are very grateful for the support we've gotten from our sponsors, who have contributed prizes for the homebrewers winning the various competitions.

![](/images/wp/2019/10/image.png)

[Vinbua in Ålesund](https://www.facebook.com/Vinbua.no/) has sponsored us with prizes. They sell homebrewing equipment and ingredients plus carnival supplies and much more. They're a great place to get hold of kveik and hop flowers.

![](/images/wp/2019/10/image-2.png)

[GRS Riflestocks](https://www.grsriflestocks.com) produces stocks for rifles and is recognized world-wide for high quality and ergonomics. They're based in Hornindal and have sponsored us with a stock.

![](/images/wp/2019/10/image-3.png)

[Brewshop](https://brewshop.no/) is a leading online shop for beer brewing, with free shipping on orders over 1300 NOK. They have a physical shop in Trondheim, but ship goods to the entire country.

![](/images/wp/2019/10/jernia.png)

[Jernia Ørsta](https://www.jernia.no/store/Jernia%20%C3%98rsta) stocks equipment and ingredients for beer brewing in addition to Jernia's regular range.

![](/images/wp/2019/10/image-4.png)

[Vestnorsk Kulturakademi](http://www.kulturakademi.no/) in Voss is a foundation working to preserve and pass on traditional hands-on knowledge. On October 25th this year they're having a [course in traditional brewing with kveik](https://www.facebook.com/events/2528076247413637/) in Voss. They sponsored the festival board's annual meeting in Voss.

![](/images/wp/2019/10/image-5.png)

[Voss Bryggeri](https://vossbryggeri.com/) is one of Norway's leading microbreweries, and the first to invest in brewing traditional ale and beer with kveik in Norway. They [are coming to the festival](/nyheter/2019-09-21-voss-bryggeri/), and also sponsored the festival board's annual meeting in Voss.
