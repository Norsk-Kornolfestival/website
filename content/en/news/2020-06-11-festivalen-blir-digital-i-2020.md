We have concluded that we cannot arrange the festival in Hornindal this year. The risk is just too high, both in terms of government conditions, health, and economically. Sorry! We are working on arranging a digital version of the festival instead, probably on the same dates. We'll announce more details when we have them.

Those who have bought tickets will be able to get a refund (minus 4% fee to Tikkio). We'll try to do the refund via Tikkio, but if it's too expensive we'll contact you individually about the refund.
