We still don't know if it will be possible to arrange the festival in October. Developments in Norway are very positive so far, but things may change before October. We don't know if it will be possible for guests to come from abroad, and our economic situation will of course also be very uncertain.

So far the board has decided to wait until the first week of June, and then see if we can come to a decision. We will post an update then.

If the festival is cancelled all tickets will be refunded, minus Tikkio's 4% fee.
