Brewshop has kindly sponsored us with gift cards for the competition winners, and we're very grateful for that.

[Brewshop](https://brewshop.no/) is a leading online store for beer brewing, with free shipping on orders over 1300 kroner (up to 35kg). Their physical store in Trondheim they call Brewshop Megastore, since it's one of the largest brewing stores in the Nordic region. They ship goods throughout the country.
