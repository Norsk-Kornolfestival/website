Everyone who comes to the festival gets a ballot they can use to vote for one beer. The judges count the votes and crown the three that received the most votes as winners.

## 2nd place: Morgedal Susle- og Ølbryggarlag

![](/images/wp/2022/10/IMG_0530-683x1024.jpg)

In shared second place came [this year's demo brewers from Morgedal in Telemark](/nyheter/2022-09-29-demo-brewers-from-telemark-halvor-terje/), Halvor Bjåland and Terje Haugen. A strong achievement for some from the other side of the country!

## 2nd place: Tore and Eirik Raftevold

![](/images/wp/2022/10/IMG_0518-1024x678.jpg)

The other second place went to Tore and Eirik Raftevold for their *Otterdal Tradisjon*, a traditional raw ale from Hornindal.

## 1st place: Olav Sverre Gausemel

![](/images/wp/2022/10/IMG_0545-1024x683.jpg)

Olav Sverre got first place for his corn ale, thus repeating the feat from last year. Very well done! The prize was a juniper box made by Jan Hall. It has "Kornølfestival" carved into the lid.
