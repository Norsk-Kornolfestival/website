![](/images/wp/2023/09/image-1-1024x768.png)**Atle Ove Martinussen (left) and Hans Geir Eiken searching for kveik.**

Hans Geir Eiken from NIBIO and Atle Ove Martinussen from the Western Norway Cultural Academy will be coming to the festival to give a talk on their project to collect kveik and search for old kveik remains, in order to learn as much as possible about kveik and its history. They recently published [a preprint of a scientific paper](https://www.biorxiv.org/content/10.1101/2023.07.03.547515v1) on the subject, so they have actual results to present.
