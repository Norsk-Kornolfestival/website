Our tickets to oppskåke have been sold out for a while, but we've been able to add 5 more tickets for the oppskåke, and we also created an "Oppskåke nr 2", a second oppskåke. Both of these are the same kind of event, but in two different places. Both are now open for sale.

If you're not familiar with what this is: it's a traditional oppskåke party in a traditional brewhouse, where we serve free kornøl and cured meats. It's held on the Thursday before the festival begins.
