Voss Bryggeri and Voss Fellesbryggeri brew beer that is mainly based on kveik from Voss. In addition, they have also brought ciders made with kveik this year.

![](/images/wp/2023/10/ll_voss_fb_logo_sort-1.png)

They have brought an impressive list of beers to the festival:

- Oregonian Pale Ale - Skiferøl Hoppy ale with juniper
- Kveika Rye Ale - Rye ale with kveik
- Porter - Porter
- Jolabrygg - Christmas ale with kveik
- Gardsøl - Farm ale with kveik
- Camping - Sharp ale with kveik
- Ljos Kos - Blonde ale with kveik
- Vossabrygg - Traditional farm ale with kveik
- Smale IPA - IPA with kveik
- Fatlagra Vossabrygg - Vossabrygg aged in whisky barrel
- Vossasider Apple - Apple cider with kveik
- Vossasider Bringebær - Raspberry cider with kveik
