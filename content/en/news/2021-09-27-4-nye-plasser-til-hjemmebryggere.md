We've been a bit cautious this year about opening up for homebrewers, since [Gro-salen](/news/2021-09-23-festival-i-gro-salen/) is smaller than the old hall, and we had rather few judges. But now we feel quite confident that we can handle a few more, so we've released 4 more tickets for homebrewers. If you want one, you should probably buy quickly, as the ones we've released so far have been gone immediately.
