There will be a kornøl festival in 2021! Everything is a bit uncertain due to the pandemic, but we're trying to arrange both a physical festival in Hornindal *and* a digital festival online.

There probably won't be as many international visitors this year, and the festival may be a bit smaller overall, but we're doing our best to put on a full festival.

Tickets are [available on Tikkio](https://tikkio.com/tickets/23286-norsk-kornolfestival-2021). If something happens and the physical part of the festival has to be cancelled, or you can't make it, we'll refund your ticket (minus the Tikkio fee).

Hotel rooms can be booked at [Hotell Raftevold](https://havilahotelraftevold.no/). The hotel has promised us a discount code, which we'll post as soon as we receive it.

Hope to see you at the festival this year!
