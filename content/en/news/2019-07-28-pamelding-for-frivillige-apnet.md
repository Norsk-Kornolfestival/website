We on the board believe that the Norsk Kornølfestival is one of the most important initiatives being done to keep Norwegian traditional farmhouse ale and kveik alive. The festival creates pride among the brewers and shows people in Nordfjord and Sunnmøre that Norwegian beer culture is important. We already have registered participants at the festival from Denmark, Sweden, Czech Republic, Germany, Great Britain, Netherlands and USA.

Running a beer festival is a lot of work, and we on the board have been working on it since November already. We're going to put in much more work before the festival in October, but we can't do it all ourselves. Do you have the time and ability to help us create pride around Norwegian beer culture? If so, we'd be very happy!

See [the volunteer page](/frivillige/) for information about tasks, what you get, registration, etc.
