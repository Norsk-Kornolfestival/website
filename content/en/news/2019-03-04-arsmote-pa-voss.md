The festival board had our annual meeting on Voss this weekend and planned this year's festival. A very successful weekend, with lots of great beer, good food, and not least good company and great stories.

We also worked on recruitment, and are hopeful that even more brewers from Voss will attend the festival this year.

**English**: The festival board had our annual meeting in Voss this weekend, planning this year's festival. We're very happy with the weekend: productive discussions, good beer and food, and even better company.

We also pressed traditional brewers from the Voss area to come to the festival, and believe there will be even more brewers from Voss at the festival this year.
