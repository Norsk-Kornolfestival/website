GRS Riflestocks has kindly sponsored us with the first prize in the Norwegian Championship in Corn Beer: a rifle stock. GRS started in 1983 as a furniture manufacturer in Hornindal, but switched to rifle stock production in 2011. Both production and headquarters are still located locally in Hornindal.

GRS is highly regarded worldwide for high quality and good ergonomic fit, and their rifle stocks are sold in the USA, Canada, New Zealand, Great Britain, South Africa, Czech Republic and many other countries.
