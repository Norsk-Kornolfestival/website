We've been very lucky this year and have gotten Sigrid Strætkvern to be our host. Sigrid works for Ringnes, Norway's biggest brewery, where she has the title "Mood Maker." She is known for being both enthusiastic and knowledgeable about beer. This summer she has been traveling around Asia giving courses within the Carlsberg system.
