Lars Marius Garshol will be doing a talk at the festival based on [his new book](https://www.garshol.priv.no/download/tradisjonsbrygging-av-ol/) (in Norwegian). The talk will describe some new discoveries about how beer was brewed in Norway, and about the early history of these beers.

There will be sales and signings of the book afterwards.
