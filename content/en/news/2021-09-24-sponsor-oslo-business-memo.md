We will have [live streaming](/news/2021-09-22-digital-festival/) of the festival this year, and [Oslo Business Memo](https://oslobusinessmemo.no/OBM/OM-OSS/OBM-Nettkringkasting) has stepped up as a collaboration partner and is producing the live broadcast from the festival. OBM's streaming team has extensive experience with live production, for example OBM produced 35 live-streamed meetings and debates over four hectic days during Arendalsuka in mid-August.

One of the reasons OBM has stepped up is probably that the CEO is Magne Otterdal. Those who know Hornindal a bit know that Otterdal is a small valley just outside Grodås, and Magne comes from there. He recently moved back to Hornindal, and that's how he learned about the festival.

We're very pleased with the support, and we believe OBM will make this year's digital festival *significantly* more professional than the 2020 broadcast. You can expect many great interviews and other features!

Broadcasts on [Friday](https://www.crowdcast.io/e/norsk-kornlfestival-2021) and [Saturday](https://www.crowdcast.io/e/norsk-kornlfestival-2021-2).
