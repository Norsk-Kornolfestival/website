This year's festival will be a bit smaller due to corona, and so we've decided to hold it in Gro-salen. This is a new event venue that has just been renovated through volunteer work by the association Grobygget. Several thousand volunteer hours went into transforming the old factory into a beautiful event hall, and we hope it can provide a more pleasant setting for the festival than the gym we've usually used.

Gro-salen is also much closer to the hotel, so it's a bit quicker to get there and back than before. The address is [Ytrehornsvegen 14](https://goo.gl/maps/dqyGfy7RCQJ7Hnay7).

See the picture below:

![](/images/wp/2021/09/image-4-1024x700.png)*Grodås center with the important locations marked.*

As you can see, the camping is also in a new location this year.

The lectures this year will take place in Kommunebygget, which is right next to Gro-bygget.

Gro-bygget also has a beautiful park outside, down towards Hornindalsvannet, which we hope the weather will allow us to use a lot. The demo brewing will take place there:

![](/images/wp/2021/09/image-3-1024x768.png)**Gro-parken with stage.**

And for those who might be confused about the naming and wondering if the festival is taking place in Gro-parken, Gro-bygget or Gro-salen, the answer is: all three!
