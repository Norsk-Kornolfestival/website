[Bjørnafjorden Bryggeri](https://bfbryggeri.no/) is coming to the festival. The brewery is located in Os south of Bergen, and was started by five homebrewing friends. One of them, Tarjei Skeie, grew up in Dyrvedalen in Voss, which is the main area for the local farmhouse brewing.

**The beers:**

- **vOSsing** 4.7% - a lighter pale beer with Voss kveik, hence the name.
- **Oppskåka** 6.5% - a kveik-fermented beer with juniper infusion.
- **Passion for kveik** 4.7% - Wheat beer with passion fruit and Voss kveik.
- **Fumadora** 6.66% - Dark, strong smoked beer.
- **Kølnial** 4.7% - Kölsch.
