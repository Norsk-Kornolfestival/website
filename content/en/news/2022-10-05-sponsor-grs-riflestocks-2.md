We're lucky to have [GRS Riflestocks](http://com/) as sponsors for this year's festival. They've donated one of their high-quality super-ergonomic riflestocks to be the first prize of the home brewing competition. GRS started in 1983 as a furniture producer in Hornindal, but switched to riflestock production in 2011. Both production and headquarters are still located here in Hornindal.

GRS is highly regarded worldwide for their high quality and excellent ergonomic design, and their riflestocks are sold in the USA, Canada, New Zealand, the United Kingdom, South Africa, the Czech Republic, and many more countries. So we're very pleased to have their support for the festival.
