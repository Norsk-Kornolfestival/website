Brewshop has been so kind as to sponsor us with gift cards for the competition winners, and we are very grateful for that.

[Brewshop](https://brewshop.no/) is a leading online shop for homebrewing, with free shipping on orders over 1500 NOK (up to 35kg). Their physical store in Trondheim they call Brewshop Megastore, as it is one of Scandinavia's largest homebrewing shops. They ship to the whole country.

Their product range includes kveik yeast from laboratories and books on traditional brewing.
