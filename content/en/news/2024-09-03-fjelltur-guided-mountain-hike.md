If you want to see a little of the nature around Hornindal we recommend joining this guided hike, which will take you up to Nakkebruna, a public cabin in the mountainside above Hornindal.

The hike takes 30 minutes at a quiet pace, up to 190 meters of height. Good footwear is recommended. At the cabin some light snacks will be served, with drink in keeping with the theme of the festival.

Those who want to can join the guides for an extra excursion a little higher in the mountainside, to a [quarry from the Neolithic/Bronze Age](https://www.kulturminnesok.no/kart/?q=&am-county=&lokenk=location&am-lok=&am-lokdating=&am-lokconservation=&am-enk=&am-enkdating=&am-enkconservation=&bm-county=&bm-municipality=&cp=1&bounds=61.977371563875245,6.50339663028717,61.97535272701305,6.5055423974990845&zoom=18&id=25918).

The hike starts from outside the festival hall at 1130 on Saturday. [Tickets at Tikkio](https://tikkio.com/tickets/43681-norsk-kornolfestival-2024).
