**Dear homebrewers and hobby brewers!**

This year we have a small extra service for you at the beer festival: free analysis of your own beer! ![](https://static.xx.fbcdn.net/images/emoji.php/v9/f4c/1/16/1f642.png):)



The guys from Vit Hva Du Drikker will come around and ask all the breweries and homebrewers if you want to have your beer analyzed...

The team from VIT HVA DU DRIKKER will have an advanced analysis device at the festival, and everyone who is a guest at the beer festival can bring their own beer and get it tested for alcohol content, OG, FG, etc.

Bring the beer in a 0.33 or 0.5 liter bottle.

Each bottle must be labeled with the owner's name, and a number if you have multiple bottles.

The service is FREE ![](https://static.xx.fbcdn.net/images/emoji.php/v9/f6c/1/16/2764.png)❤

So just go to the guys in the white coats if you have any questions....
