Do you want to stay at the historic hotel [Visnes](https://www.visneshotel.no/)? Kornølfestivalen is grateful that Visnes Hotel wants to collaborate with us, and we've managed to get a good price for our festival participants at 1250 NOK per night. The hotel is built in Swiss chalet style and is part of the De Historiske Hotell chain.

Contact: [post@visneshotel.no](mailto:post@visneshotel.no) A shuttle bus will be set up between Hornindal and Stryn during the festival.
