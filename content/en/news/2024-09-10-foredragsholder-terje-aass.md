Terje Aass is well known as the former director of Aass Brewery in Drammen. After retiring as director, he has been an enthusiastic brewer at Aass's microbrewery in Drammen.

What is perhaps less well known is that Terje has also had a longstanding fascination with traditional Norwegian beer and beer culture. He has now agreed to come to the Norwegian Kornøl Festival to speak about a topic that's close to his heart: "**Beer is culture!**"

We're really looking forward to it!
