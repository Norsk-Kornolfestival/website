![](/images/wp/2021/10/image-1024x683.png)**Olav Sverre Gausemel receives the first prize.**

The audience voted on their favorite beers, and the results were as follows.

**1st place:** Olav Sverre Gausemel, Konnjøl (beer #1)

Prize: Gift card worth 2500,- and a Teku glass from [Brewshop](https://brewshop.no/)

**2nd place:** Arvid Solheim, Maltøl (beer #15)

Prize: Kegland mobile keg from [Humleloftet](https://www.humleloftet.no/)

**3rd place:** Terje Raftevold, Kornøl (beer #41)

Prize: Gift card worth 500,- from [Humleloftet](https://www.humleloftet.no/)
