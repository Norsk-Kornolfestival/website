Camping tickets are now [available](https://tikkio.com/tickets/8125-norsk-kornolfestival-2019). If you're coming with a camping wagon or motor home, you can buy a camping ticket (with or without electricity) and stay on the campground Friday-Saturday of the festival. The campground is 100 meters from the festival. Toilet facilities are available.

Festival entrance must be purchased separately.

**English**: Camping tickets are [now available](https://tikkio.com/tickets/8125-norsk-kornolfestival-2019). These let you park your camping wagon or motor home near the festival hall. Entrance to the festival must be purchased separately.
