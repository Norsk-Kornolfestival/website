To run the festival, we are completely dependent on the volunteers who work to keep the festival running. We need volunteers who can work at the door, help in the kitchen, and be cleaning crew. Volunteers get meals and drinks covered at the festival, and get free admission the day they don't work.

[More details and registration here](/frivillige/).

A big thank you to everyone who helps!
