Traveling by bus to Kornølfestivalen can be efficient, comfortable and cheap. E39 passes through Hornindal, but from the larger cities, there are better bus connections to Stryn where Kornølfestivalen will set up a shuttle to and from the festival.

Note that bus ticket prices get more expensive the closer you get to departure date.

Vy.no has the direct buses, and here you can find the local buses: [Bus | Frammr.no](https://frammr.no/buss)
