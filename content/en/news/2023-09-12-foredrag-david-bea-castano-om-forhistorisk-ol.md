David Bea Castaño will be coming to the festival to do a talk on prehistoric beers in southern Europe. His focus will be the area between the Alps and the Mediterranean.

David Bea Castaño is a researcher and archaeologist at the University of Rovira i Virgili outside Barcelona in Spain. He is also a certified beer sommelier, and has collaborated with breweries to brew recreations of prehistoric beers.
