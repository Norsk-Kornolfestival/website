Traditional brewing is a prerequisite for Eik & Tid's very existence, and Kornølfestivalen is their yearly pilgrimage.

They are looking forward to Norway's most important beer festival and are bringing the following beers:

- Kurant - barrel-aged pale sour ale with blackcurrant- Juleangst - barrel-aged brown sour ale with blackcurrant- Skrekkelig Jul - barrel-aged brown sour ale with raspberry- Jule III - barrel-aged golden sour ale with pine, spruce, and juniper- Lazarus - barrel-aged brown sour ale with redcurrant and honey
