![](/images/wp/2019/09/sigurd-1024x775.jpg)**Sigurd Johan Saure (image from *[Kveik Training](https://www.kveiktraining.com/about-us))*

Sigurd Johan comes from the farm Tormodgarden in Sykkylven, roughly 85km from Hornindal. Sykkylven is quite close to Ålesund airport. He learned to brew kornøl from his grandfather and uncle. He is the owner of kveik [#8 Tormodgarden](http://www.garshol.priv.no/download/farmhouse/kveik.html#kv8), and has started offering courses in kornøl brewing under the name [Kveik Training](https://www.kveiktraining.com/).

Sigurd Johan will be serving a porter fermented with kveik and a traditional raw kornøl with kveik and juniper infusion.
