## Practical information

### Getting there

**By air:** The main airport is Ålesund (Vigra). A festival bus runs on Thursday at ~16:50 (after the 16:05 and 16:20 flights land). Closer airport: Ørsta/Volda.

**By bus:** Local bus via [Frammr](https://frammr.no/) (stop at "Grodås"). Long-distance bus via [Vy](https://www.vy.no/) (Oslo–Hornindal).

**Return bus:** Sunday at 09:30 from the hotel to Ålesund airport (connects to 13:20 flight).

**Travel packages:** Available on [Tikkio](https://tikkio.com/events/60613-norsk-kornolfestival-2025) including hotel + airport bus + breakfast.

### The venue

The festival takes place at **Honndalshallen** in Hornindal, Møre og
Romsdal, Norway.

**Opening hours:**
- Friday: 17:00–00:30
- Saturday: 12:00–01:00
