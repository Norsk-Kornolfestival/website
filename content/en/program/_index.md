## Program 2026

The festival runs over four days, from Thursday October 1 to Sunday October 4. The main festival is Friday and Saturday.

### Thursday, October 1

| Time | Event |
|------|-------|
| ~16:50 | Bus from Vigra (Ålesund) airport to Hornindal |
| 19:30–22:00 | **Oppskåke** — opening celebration in historic farmhouse building (separate ticket). Meet at Raftevold Hotel. |

### Friday, October 2

| Time | Event |
|------|-------|
| 10:00–16:00 | **Traditional farmhouse brewing** in a 17th-century eldhus. Led by Stig Seljeset (kveik #22 Stålljen). Juniper infusion, mashing, lautering, cooling, kveik pitching (with traditional screaming). No boiling (raw ale). Includes traditional cured meats, flatbread lunch, and kornøl. *(Separate ticket: Brewing Pass)* |
| 11:00–15:00 | **Women-only brewing session** *(separate ticket)* |
| 17:00 | Doors open |
| 18:00 | Official festival opening |
| 20:00 | Home brewers pack up; commercial breweries begin sales |
| 00:30 | Festival closes |

### Saturday, October 3

| Time | Event |
|------|-------|
| 11:30 | Optional guided hike to Nakkebruna cabin (190m elevation, ~30 min). Optional extension to Neolithic/Bronze Age quarry. |
| 12:00 | Festival opens |
| 12:00 | **Øystein Menes** demonstrates beer brewing (Sogn tradition, 40 years experience) |
| 12:00 | **Rolf Taraldset** (master wood carver) demonstrates traditional ølbolle carving |
| 14:30 | **Geir Grønnesby**: "What is the connection between the establishment of the farm and beer brewing?" — archaeological brewing stones, Iron Age |
| 17:00 | **Xavi Tortosa**: "Tradition Reimagined: Applying Brewing Heritage in Modern Craft" |
| 18:00 | Home brewers pack up; commercial breweries resume |
| 18:45 | **Festival dinner** at Raftevold Hotel — sheep's head (saudehaud), boiled potatoes, rutabaga mash, Nordfjord pancakes. Three styles of traditional ale: Hornindal konnjøl, Voss homebrew, Stjørdalsøl. *(Separate ticket)* |
| 19:00 | **Christina Wade**: "Drinking with the Dead: Women, Ale, and Ritual in Viking-Age Ireland" |
| 21:00 | Competition winners announced |
| 01:00 | Festival closes |

### TBA (during festival)

- **Jan Steensels** (VIB-KU Leuven): "Analysis of 1760 strains of farmhouse yeast" — unpublished major genetic study
- **Lars Marius Garshol**: "The Medieval Water Myth" — did people drink beer because water was unsafe?

### Sunday, October 4

| Time | Event |
|------|-------|
| 09:30 | Bus to Ålesund airport (connects to 13:20 flight) |
