## Norwegian Championship in Traditional Kornøl

The Norwegian Kornøl Festival hosts an annual Norwegian Championship in traditional kornøl (tradisjonsøl).

### Rules

- Single category: **traditional Norwegian ale (tradisjonsøl)**
- Excluded: sour beer, heavily dark-malted beers, prominent American hop character
- Max **2 beers** per brewer for judging; unlimited beers for public tasting
- All beers eligible for **public favorite** voting
- Registration via Home Brewer Ticket
