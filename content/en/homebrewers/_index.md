Home brewers sign up by purchasing a home brewer ticket. The ticket
serves as registration and grants access for two people. Each brewer
is expected to bring at least 19 liters of beer in total.

## Norwegian Championship in Traditional Kornøl

The Norwegian Kornøl Festival hosts an annual Norwegian Championship
in traditional kornøl (tradisjonsøl).

This year there is only one category: **tradisjonsøl** (traditional ale).

We don't set strict limits for the category, but the beer should taste
like Norwegian traditional ale. Sour beer, heavily dark malt, or a
distinct American hop character clearly fall outside the category.

Each brewer may enter a maximum of **1 beer** in the judging
competition, but may bring unlimited beers for the **audience
favorite** voting.

Questions can be sent to [post@norskkornolfestival.no](mailto:post@norskkornolfestival.no)
