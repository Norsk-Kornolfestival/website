Rodebak ble startet av Carl Christensen på gården Rotbakken (Rodebak er nok gammel stavemåte) på Innset i sør-Trøndelag.

**English: **Rodebak is a brewery started by Carl Christensen on the farm Rodebak (modern spelling "Rotbakken") in central Norway.

The beers:

- Rodebak Kveik, 5.5% - a pale farmhouse ale with Belgian 2-row malts, and Norwegian spelt, wheat, and oats. Fermented with #1 Sigmund.- Biere de Garde, 6.5% - brewed with Norwegian malts from Bonsak and kölsch yeast.- Mundus Novus Saison, 6.5% - hoppy saison fermented with the house strain.- De Truseløse Trippel, 8.5% - Norwegian Bonsak malts and house strain.- Bourbon Barrel-aged Biere de Garde, 7.0% - aged on Heaven Hills Bourbon Barrels.
