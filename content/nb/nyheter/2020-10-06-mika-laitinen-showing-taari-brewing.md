[Mika Laitinen](https://www.brewingnordic.com/) will appear in the festival stream to show how Finnish taari was brewed.

Mika is a Finnish author and home-brewer who has written several books on traditional brewing. His most recent is [Viking-Age Brew: ](https://www.brewingnordic.com/books/)**[ ](https://www.brewingnordic.com/books/)**[The Craft of Brewing Sahti Farmhouse Ale](https://www.brewingnordic.com/books/).

Taari was a form of farmhouse ale brewed in eastern Finland, using the great Russian-style ovens. It is related to Lithuanian keptinis. Mika will give a demonstration of how it was done at 19:50 Norwegian time.
